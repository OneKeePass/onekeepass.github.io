OneKeePass Privacy Policy

Summary

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application OneKeePass Password Manager (“Application”). The Application is a client side application.The contents of your password database is yours and is never collected by us. OneKeePass stores this data on a location chosen by the user and encrypted in the Keepass database format.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The application does not collect any of the personal identifiable information. Nothing is collected or shared with any external party
